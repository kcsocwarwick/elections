Elections 2023

Why be a Part of the KCSOC Team?


KCSOC is unlike any other society at university. Feeding our spiritual needs and (bellies!) and taking people on a journey of self-realisation is a big part of the KCSOC mission.

By being a part of the team, you'll gain a deeper sense of who you are along with other like-minded people. It will be a chance to make a difference by bringing the philosophy of KC into their lives. We can't wait for you to be part of this amazing family!


Application Process


  1. Have a look at the roles, choose up to 2 positions, and apply below.
  2. In week 9, on the 9th of March, we will hold elections instead of the usual Thursday talk. You will have an opportunity to give a short 2 minute presentation. We're looking for you to discuss:

    • Why you became a part of KCSOC
    • Why you have applied to the role
    • What are you looking to gain from being part of the team
    • Anything else you want to add

We'll also ask you a couple questions after your presentation, but it will be super casual, so nothing to worry about! If you'd like to have slides, please send them to: kcsoc.warwick.elections@gmail.com

Application Deadline and Results


Please fill in the application form by 11:59pm on Saturday 4th March Please also make sure that you're available on election day on the 9th!

The aim of this election is to put together the best exec to take KCSOC into the next year, so please do apply! We can't wait to welcome you into the team!

Voting will take place in person on the day.

Roles

President

Why Apply?

If you have a vision of leading KCSOC to success, and are ready to make some serious progression in your spiritual life, then this is your opportunity. KCSOC will constantly engage you in weekly events that will require tremendous amounts of planning, organisation skills, time-management, and teamwork. Things never run smoothly, but it means you'll pick up invaluable skills overcoming those hurdles.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Oversees all of the society's exec, activities, promotion and more!
  • Point of contact for KCSOC National Team and Ambassador
  • A good role model of KCSOC

Vice-President

Why Apply?

If you're motivated to give our members the best possible experience then this is your opportunity. There are lots of things to sort out for our events to run smoothly and make sure our members have the best time possible, so you're the key to KCSOC success!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Support the president in their role
  • Contacting and building relationships with speakers
  • Running our main Thursday events
  • Organise any social and collab events
  • Stay on top of communication from the SU

Secretary

Why Apply?

The Secretary is an essential role that acts as the key support for the entire society. Your logistical skills are needed for our smooth running and to ensure you keep all exec and members up to date with upcoming events. You are passionate about paying attention to every detail.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Booking rooms for events (must be done well in advance!)
  • Take feedback from our members and minutes during meetings whilst following up with relevant exec to make sure points are implemented
  • Manage and keep log of inventory in lockers
  • Keep our announcement slides and SU page up to date with the latest information
  • Stay on top of various admin like member sign up forms, frep applications and more

Treasurer

Why Apply?

Although KCSOC says not to be attached to money, we still need it to help the society run and grow! The treasurer has the crucial role of looking after the society's finances. If you are the type of person who wants to get the maximum out of everything, then this is the role for you!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Keep track of all monetary movement e.g. spending on events, food shopping, and donations
  • Apply for any university funding which is available & expensing things through the SU
  • Organising transport for retreats (with the help of the President and KCSOC National Team)
  • Come up with exciting fund raising ideas if the society needs money!

Welcome Team

Why Apply?

KCSOC is praised for being an approachable society that has a friendly, loving atmosphere and the welcome team is essential in creating this! Your great public speaking skills will be needed to deliver key information as well as your natural warmth as you make sure unfamiliar faces feel welcome, as well as making sure the regular faces feel loved and cared for!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Being an approachable, friendly face, welcoming and talking to our members
  • Writing event descriptions
  • MCing our events which includes:
    • Starting the session with an icebreaker
    • Introducing the speaker
    • Announcements at the end

Social Media Officer

Why Apply?

Social media is the main way people know about out events, so this is a really vital role. You'll be in charge of maintaining our pages and engaging with the audience. It is a great way of spiritualising social media for both yourself and others!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Take lots of pictures during our events
  • Keep our WhatsApp groupchat and Instagram engaging and up to date with event info and pictures
  • Write descriptions for events

Digital Content Creator

Why Apply?

Often people come to KCSOC or decide to come to an event because they have seen some eye-catching posters, videos on the piazza screen, or information on TVs around campus! You'll strive to make content that catches people's attention and gets them excited to join us

Roles and responsibilities

  • Designing posters for weekly events
  • Creating talk summaries and photo collages to post on our social media
  • Promoting flashship events and the retreat all over campus!

Sports Coordinator

Why Apply?

Exercise is such an important part of our lives and a great way to connect with our members. By taking good care of our body, it becomes a lot easier to take good care of our mind and soul too! This year, we have been playing netball, but you'll also have the opportunity to develop KCSOC sport as you wish!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Lead the weekly sport sessions
  • Encourage a positive, supportive environment for everyone to take part
  • Book courts for each week
  • Look after the netballs and other sports equipment

Tech Lead

Why Apply?

You're a natural when it comes to technology and there's no shortage of tech tasks to be done for our events to run smoothly and it's the most effective way of making sure our members are reminded about events.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Send timely email and whatsapp reminders to all members
  • Set up background music and announcement slides for our events
  • Assist speaker and MCs with projector and volume controls

SU & Chaplaincy Co-ordinator

Why Apply?

A big part of KCSOC's success is all about having a good relationship with the Student Union, Chaplaincy etc. It's important we make sure we're on top of any requirements we need to meet so that we can ask for their help or support when we need.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Keep track of emails from the SU and chaplaincy and communicate with the rest of the exec as required
  • Attend any mandatory trainings and meetings
  • Help other exec with getting access to chaplaincy lockers and other facilities
  • Stay on top of relevant admin like SU handover, memberships, elections, society fairs, etc.

Ambassador

Why Apply?

Being always open to all people is what makes KCSOC so special, and so it's important that we are constantly spreading the word about it and getting people to check us out. You can use your amazing networking skills to let to let the whole university know about KCSOC! Many of our key exec and members found out about kcsoc through word-of-mouth.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Promote our society as widely as possible
  • Tell everyone you meet about the best society on campus

Inventory Lead

Why Apply?

As you know we have a ton of merch, equipment, instruments etc. that we need across our variety of events over each week. KCSOC needs your organizational skills to manage and look after it all.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Bring items to and from the lockers in the chaplaincy for Thursday events (or liaise other exec)
  • Keep the lockers tidy and organized
  • Keep track of the inventory to ensure we know what we have
  • Stock up cutlery, whiteboard pens etc.

Support Team

Why Apply?

There's always lots and lots to be done for KCSOC to run smoothly, and its often the case that members of the team will need help with carrying out their responsibilities. You'll be the backbone of the entire team, providing support to any other role within the society! You will also be in charge of some essential tasks on the day of the event!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Helping set up and run the Thursday events
  • Assist all other roles in the team with their responsibilities
  • Being a friendly face for our members
