Why be a Part of the KCSOC Team?


KCSOC is unlike any other society at university. Feeding our spiritual needs and taking people on a journey of self-realisation is a big part of the KCSOC mission.

By being a part of the team, you'll gain a deeper sense of who you are along with other like-minded people. It will be a chance to make a difference by bringing the philosophy of KC into their lives. We can't wait for you to be part of this amazing family!


Application Process


  1. Have a look at the roles, choose up to 2 positions, and apply below.
  2. In the last week of term, on the 17th of March, we will hold elections instead of the usual Thursday talk. You will have an opportunity to give a short 2 minute presentation. We're looking for you to discuss:

    • Why you became a part of KCSOC
    • Why you have applied to the role
    • What are you looking to gain from being part of the team
    • Anything else you want to add

We'll also ask you a couple questions after your presentation, but it will be super casual, so nothing to worry about! If you'd like to have slides, please send them to: kcsoc.warwick.elections@gmail.com

Application Deadline and Results


Please fill in the application form by Monday 14th March Please also make sure that you're available on election day on the 17th!

The aim of this election is to put together the best exec to take KCSOC into the next year, so please do apply! We can't wait to welcome you into the team!

Roles

Presidential Penguin

(President)

Why Apply?

If you have a vision of leading KCSOC to success, and are ready to make some serious progression in your spiritual life, then this is your opportunity. KCSOC will constantly engage you in weekly events that will require tremendous amount of planning, organisation skills, time-management as well as teamwork. Things never run smoothly, but it means you'll pick up invaluable skills overcoming those hurdles.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Oversees all of the society's activities
  • Contacting and building relationships with speakers
  • Point of contact for KCSOC National Team and Ambassador
  • A good role model of ISKCON

Vice-Presidential Puffin

(Vice President)

Why Apply?

If you're motivated to give our members the best possible experience then this is your opportunity. You'll be the person running our main Thursday events! There are lots of things to sort out for our events to run smoothly and make sure our members have the best time possible, so you're the key to KCSOC success!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Support the president in their role
  • Responsible for running Thursday events which involves:
    • Leading and co-ordinating with exec to ensure the room is ready before event start time
    • Co-ordinate with welcome team to prepare icebreakers and announcements
    • Lead and organize team cooking efforts for events in case of no external source of free food (you don't personally need to be a great cook, but it's a bonus!)
    • Organize sufficient snacks, drinks, cutlery
    • Lead and organize our games/social events

Secretarial Owl

(Secretary)

Why Apply?

The Secretary is an essential role that acts as the key support for the entire society. Your organisation skills are needed to ensure you know where all our merch is, and keep all exec and members up to date with upcoming events. You are passionate about making sure KCSOC keeps improving and pushing boundaries.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Booking rooms for events (must be done well in advance!)
  • Writing minutes during meetings and following up with relevant exec to make sure points are implemented
  • Manage and keep log of inventory in lockers
  • Work with the VP and treasurer, ensuring the society doesn't run short on supplies for events

Counting Squirrel

(Treasurer)

Why Apply?

Although KCSOC says not to be attached to money, we still need it to help the society run and grow! The treasurer has the crucial role of looking after the society's finances. If you are the type of person who wants to get the maximum out of everything, then this is the role for you!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Keep track of all monetary movement e.g. spending on events, food shopping, and donations
  • Apply for any university funding which is available & expensing things through the SU
  • Organising transport for retreats (with the help of the President and KCSOC National Team)
  • Come up with exciting fund raising ideas if the society needs money!

Welcome Butterfly

(Welcome Team)

Why Apply?

KCSOC is praised for being an approachable society that has a friendly, loving atmosphere and the welcome butterfly is essential in creating this! Your great public speaking skills will be needed to make sure unfamiliar faces feel welcome, as well as making sure the regular faces feel loved and cared for!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Being an approachable, friendly face, welcoming and talking to our members
  • Writing event descriptions
  • MCing our events which includes:
    • Starting the session with an icebreaker
    • Introducing the speaker
    • Announcements at the end

Social Media Wizard

(Social Media Officer)

Why Apply?

Social media is the main way people know about out events, so this is a really vital role. You'll be in charge of maintaining our pages and engaging with the audience. It is a great way of spiritualising social media for both yourself and others!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Take lots of pictures during our events
  • Keep all our social media platforms up to date with upcoming events which includes:
    • Instagram posts & stories
    • Facebook events
    • Emails
    • WhatsApp

Marketing Magpie

(Marketing Officer)

Why Apply?

The people are what makes KCSOC so special, and so it's important that we are constantly spreading the word about it and getting people to check us out, whether it be through eye-catching posters, videos on the piazza screen, or through word of mouth! You'll strive to let the whole university hear about KCSOC!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Designing posters and other promo materials for all our different events
  • Leading new and innovative marketing initiatives with the support from the whole team
  • Organising collabs with other societies to spread the word about KCSOC
  • Promoting our national retreats across the university

Sports Shark

(Sports Coordinator)

Why Apply?

Exercise is such an important part of our lives and a great way to connect with our members. By taking good care of our body, it becomes a lot easier to take good care of our mind and soul too! This year, we have been playing netball, but you'll also have the opportunity to develop KCSOC sport as you wish!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Lead the weekly sport sessions
  • Encourage a positive environment for everyone to take part
  • Book courts for each week
  • Look after the netballs and other sports equipment

Support Ninja

(Support Team)

Why Apply?

There's always lots and lots to be done for KCSOC to run smoothly, and its often the case that members of the team will need help with carrying out their responsibilities. You'll be the backbone of the entire team, providing support to any other role within the society! You will also be in charge of some essential tasks on the day of the event!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Helping set up and run the Thursday events
  • Assist all other roles in the team with their responsibilities
  • Being a friendly face for our members
