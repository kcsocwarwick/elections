KCSOC Warwick
Elections 2021

Why be a Part of the KCSOC Team?

KCSOC is unlike any other society at university. Feeding our spiritual needs and taking people on a journey of self-realisation is a big part of the KCSOC mission. Not only will you be starting your own, personal journey of self—discovery; you will be making a powerful change in the lives of others too!

We’ve seen countless young people completely transform after joining KCSOC over the years and now they’re out there in the big wide world contributing to society in massive and previously unthinkable ways. By being apart of the team, you’ll gain a deeper sense of who you are and will also make lifelong friends with like-minded people. You’ll also soon find a greater vision for your life unfolding that you wouldn’t have been able to tap into otherwise.

Being part of this new exec team will be a chance to make a difference by bringing the philosophy of kc into their lives. We can't wait for you to be part of this exciting team!

Application Process

The application process is very quick and simple!

  1. Have a look at roles, choose up to 2 positions, and apply below. SU Membership is not essential to apply or vote. As long as you are a familiar face at KCSOC, then you are more than welcome to come take part!
  2. In the last week (17th March 2022) we will hold elections instead of the usual Thursday talk. You will have an opportunity to give a short presentation (~2mins). We're looking for you to discuss:
    • Why you have applied to the role and what you can bring to the role
    • Why you become a part of KCSOC
    • What are you looking to gain from being part of the team
    • Anything else you think is relevant!
    We'll also ask you a couple questions after your presentation, but it will be super casual, so nothing to worry! If you'd like to have slides, please send them to: kcsoc.warwick.elections@gmail.com

Application Deadline and Results

Videos must be sent to kcsoc.warwick.elections@gmail.com by Thursday 11 March 2021 (Week 9). If your video is too big to send via email, use a google drive or YouTube link.

We will begin releasing these videos to our WhatsApp members chat from Friday 12th March where everyone can vote for each position.

The aim of this election is to put together the best team to take KCSOC back to campus, therefore even if you are on the current exec and want to continue then you must run!

Roles

President

Why Apply?

If you have a vision of leading KCSOC to success, and are ready to make some serious progression in your spiritual life, then this is your opportunity. KCSOC will constantly engage you in weekly events that will require tremendous amount of planning, organisation skills, time-management as well as teamwork. Things never run smoothly, but it means you’ll pick up invaluable skills overcoming those hurdles.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Oversees all the society’s committee, members, events and engagements
  • Requires dedication and good organisation skills
  • Contacting and building relationships with speakers
  • Point of contact for KCSOC National Team and Ambassador
  • A good role model of ISKCON

Vice-President

Why Apply?

If you're motivated to give our members the best possible experience then this is your opportunity. You'll be the person running our main Thursday events! There are lots of things to sort out for our events to run smoothly and make sure our members have the best time possible, so you're the key to KCSOC success!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Support the president in their role
  • Responsible for running Thursday events which involves:
    • Leading and co-ordinating with exec to ensure the room is ready before event start time
    • Co-ordinate with welcome team to prepare icebreakers and announcements
    • Lead and organize team cooking efforts for events in case of no external source of free food (you don't persoanlly need to be a great cook, but it's a bonus!)
    • Organize sufficient snacks, drinks, cutlery
    • Lead and organize our games/social events

Secretary

Why Apply?

The Secretary is an essential role that acts as the key support for the entire society. Your organisation skills are needed to ensure you know where all our merch is, and keep all exec and members are up to date with upcoming events. You are passionate about making sure KCSOC keeps improving and pushing boundaries.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Booking rooms for events (must be done well in advance!)
  • Writing minutes during meetings and following up with relevant exec to make sure points are implemented
  • Keep up to date with food related inventory numbers i.e. cutlery requirements (cups, plates, spoons)
  • Manage and keep log of inventory in lockers
  • Work in close coordination with the VP and treasurer, ensuring the society doesn’t run short on supplies for events

Treasurer

Why Apply?

Although KCSoc says not to be attached to money, we still need it to help the society run and grow! You’ll be in charge of all finances, will also get to decide what we should invest in. Yoga mats? Instruments? Cool lights? Your call!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Keep track of all monetary movement e.g. spending on events, donations
  • Apply for any university funding which is available & expensing things through the SU
  • Organising transport for retreats (with help of President and KCSOC National Team)
  • Come up with exciting fund raising ideas if the society needs money!

Welcome Team & Hosts (2 Places)

Why Apply?

KCSoc is praised for being an approachable society that has a friendly, loving atmosphere. As part of the welcome team, you will be playing a big part in creating this atmosphere! Your great social skills will be needed to make sure unfamiliar faces feel welcome, as well as making sure the regular faces feel loved and cared for. Keep the positive vibe in KCSoc going!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Be an approachable, friendly face - welcome and talk to our members
  • Natural points of contact for members
  • Get contact details of all new attendees
  • MC our events which includes:
    • Start the session with an icebreaker
    • Introduce the speaker
    • Announcements at the end

Social Media

Why Apply?

Social media is vital as it's how we let people know about our events, so this is a really important role. If your caption game is strong and you’re a social media junkie, then this is for you!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Take lots of pictures during our events
  • Keep all our social media platforms up to date with upcoming events which includes:
    • Instagram posts & stories
    • Facebook events
    • Emails
    • WhatsApp

Mantra & Music

Why Apply?

Meditation is a key component to the KCSoc experience. If you’re passionate about your music and value the positive impact of meditation, this is a perfect fit for you!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Organising fun-filled, all-inclusive music jam sessions on a weekly basis
  • Organising daily japa meditation sessions
  • Coordinating room bookings with the Secretary for meditation sessions and the weekly Music Jams
  • Manage the KCSOC Music Instagram page

Marketing & Outreach

Why Apply?

The people are what makes KCSoc so special, and so it's important that we are constantly spreading the word about it and getting people to check us out. You’ll strive to let the whole university hear about KCSoc!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Designing posters and other promo material e.g. videos
  • Getting posters printed, leading distribution on campus
  • Point of contact for SU and ensure KCSOC abides by SU regulations
  • Take charge of the specific SU society duties including the management of the members’ Welfare and Safety.
  • Thinking outside the box about new initiatives to attract more people to come to KCSoc such as collabs
  • Promoting our national retreats across the university e.g. on the Piazza screen

Sports

Why Apply?

Exercise is such an important part of our lives and a great way to connect with our members. By taking good care of our bodies, it becomes a lot easier to take good care of our mind and soul too!

Roles and responsibilities

  • Ideally, you already plan to take a sports membership
  • Encourage a positive for environment for everyone to take part
  • On a weekly basis ensure sports bookings are made
  • Look after rackets and any needed equipment
  • Commit to attending the sports sessions regularly

Support

Why Apply?

There’s always lots to be done for KCSoc to run smoothly, and its often the case that members of the team will need help with carrying out their responsibilities. You’ll be the backbone of the entire team, providing support to any other role within the society! You will also be in charge of some essential tasks on the day of the event!

Roles and responsibilities

  • You will be in charge of:
    • Putting up the banner on the day of the event
    • Creatively setting up the KCSoc table with books, merch, etc
    • Clean up after the event
    • Making sure the banner, books, merch, etc are stored in the appropriate place
    • Help with team cooking efforts
  • Assist all other roles in the team with their responsibilities
  • Identifying and preventing any general problems
